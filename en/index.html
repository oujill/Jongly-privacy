Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

At Jongly, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Authentication & Identity

Jongly offers Google, Apple, and Guest sign-in options:

  • We only obtain an anonymous unique identifier (UID) through third-party authorization to link your game records to your account.
  • We do not access your contacts, personal emails, or social profiles.
  • Guest data is stored locally on your device and cannot be synced across devices if the app is deleted.

2. Data Storage & API Synchronization

When you use the synchronization feature, Jongly stores the following data on our secure servers via API:

  • Game Records: Scores, ranks, and specific points (e.g., Self-draw, Win types).
  • Player Nicknames: Names you manually input for your playgroup (e.g., Yi, Huo, Jiu, Lin).
  • This data is used solely to generate your "Performance Analytics" (such as radar charts and trends) and history logs.
