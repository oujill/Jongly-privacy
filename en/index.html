Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Welcome to Jongly. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data:

1. Authentication & Identity

Jongly offers Google and Apple sign-in options solely to create your unique anonymous identifier. This ensures your backup data remains correctly linked to your account. We do not collect or store your real name, email address, or phone number.

2. Data Storage & Sync

When you use the synchronization feature, your game records (such as scores, points, and player nicknames) are encrypted and transferred to our secure cloud server for backup. This service is used only to allow you to restore records when switching devices.
